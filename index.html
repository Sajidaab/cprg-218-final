Who we are

At sns photography, we are more than just photographers; we are two sisters, Sajida and Sarah, driven by an unwavering passion for the art of capturing moments. Our journey began in 2019, when we discovered photography as not just a hobby, but as a profound calling. Since then, we have dedicated ourselves to honing our craft, transforming our passion into a profession. Through our lens, we strive to tell stories, evoke emotions, and freeze fleeting moments in time. Every click of the shutter is a testament to our dedication to excellence and our commitment to preserving memories that last a lifetime. With creativity as our compass and authenticity as our guide, we embark on every shoot with passion, precision, and a relentless pursuit of perfection. Join us on this enchanting journey through the lens, where every frame captures the essence of life's most precious moments, beautifully preserved for generations to come.
